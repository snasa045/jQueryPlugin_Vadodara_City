Vadodara City, India

Vadodara is the Cultural Capital of Gujarat where all traditional festivals are celebrated in a unique way. Vadodara is one of India's most cosmopolitan cities. Vadodara has a vibrant history related to Art and Architecture. Since the era of Royal Gaekwad family, it has been a hub of Arts and Literature. Hence, it has been bestowed the title of "Kala Nagari". Vadodara is famous for variety of street food dishes, specially "Sev-Usal" is the famous one.

Check out all the Wildlife

The Sayaji Baug Zoo , situated on both the banks of the Vishwamitri river. The zoo offers 167 types of 1103 animals of various sizes and shapes. Asiatic lions are the most popular of the attractions. Sayaji Baug also has a bird zoo which has many different types of birds.

An aquarium was added to the zoo in 1962. It contains 45 species of fish.
